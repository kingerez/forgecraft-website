Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?

Your progress is automatically saved to the cloud when you sign in with Google or Apple. Make sure you're connected to the internet for your progress to sync.

Can I play on multiple devices?

Yes! Sign in with the same Google or Apple account on all your devices, and your progress will sync automatically.

How does the Auction House work?

The Auction House lets you buy and sell items with other players. List your items with a starting price and duration, and other players can bid or buy them instantly.

What are blueprints?

Blueprints are recipes for crafting equipment. You can find them while harvesting resources, buy them from the vendor, or trade for them in the Auction House. Research blueprints to learn new crafting recipes permanently.

How do I get better tools?

Craft better tools using resources and learned blueprints. Higher rarity tools harvest faster and give bonus yields. You can also buy equipment from other players in the Auction House.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please email us at support@forgecraft.app with your request. We will process your request within 30 days.

Report a Bug
