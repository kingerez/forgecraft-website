Game Features

⛏

Harvest Resources

Gather wood, stone, clay, and flax from various resource nodes. Upgrade your tools to harvest faster and discover rare materials.

🔧

Craft Equipment

Use your resources to craft powerful pickaxes, axes, shovels, and sickles. Each piece of equipment has unique bonuses.

📜

Research Blueprints

Discover and research ancient blueprints to unlock new crafting recipes. Build research stations to speed up your discoveries.

🏢

Global Auction House

Trade resources, equipment, and blueprints with players worldwide. Set your prices and build your trading empire.

🏆

Daily Quests

Complete daily challenges to earn bonus rewards. New quests available every day to keep your adventure fresh.

🌏

World Events

Participate in limited-time events with unique rewards. Compete with other players for exclusive items and glory.
